Our Goals

Create an Esprit de Corps

within the MTE. By organizing events outside of the academic context, we hope to reinforce the friendships students create.

Promote the MTE

inside and outside EPFL. The MTE program is an amazing program that combines both technical and bussiness courses. By organizing events on- and off-campus we hope to bring more attention to the MTE.

Network with professionals

in particular MTE alumni. We want to help MTE students broaden their professional network. By organizing events with the help of MTE alumni we hope to make them discover other industries and ways of working.
